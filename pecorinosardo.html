Pecorino Sardo

Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin:Italy
  • Milk Type:Sheep’s milk
  • Texture:Semi-hard to hard
  • Fat Content:Around 36%
  • Color:Pale yellow
  • Rind:Natural or waxed
  • Aging:2 months (mild) to over 8 months (aged)

About Pecorino Sardo

Pecorino Sardo is a classic Sardinian cheese made from pure sheep’s milk. It has a rich, savory flavor that ranges from mild and buttery when young to sharp and spicy when aged. Its firm texture makes it great for grating or slicing, and it’s a staple in many traditional Italian dishes.

Pairings

Pecorino Sardo pairs wonderfully with olives, honey, pears, and cured meats. It’s also excellent grated over pasta, soups, or salads. For drinks, try it with full-bodied red wines like Cannonau or Chianti.
