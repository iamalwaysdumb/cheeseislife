Monterey Jack Cheese

Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin:United States
  • Milk Type:Cow’s milk
  • Texture:Soft and smooth
  • Fat Content:Around 33%
  • Color:Pale yellow and white
  • Rind:None
  • Aging:Aged from a few weeks to several months

About Monterey Jack Cheese

Monterey Jack is a mild, creamy American cheese originally developed in California. It has a smooth, springy texture and a buttery, slightly tangy flavor that becomes sharper as it ages. It melts easily, making it perfect for dishes like quesadillas, burgers, and casseroles. There are also spicy versions, such as Pepper Jack, made with chili peppers for extra heat.

Pairings

Monterey Jack pairs well with tortilla chips, guacamole, and roasted vegetables. It also complements fruits like apples and pears and goes nicely with light beers, white wines, or sparkling cider.
