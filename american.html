Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin: United States
  • Milk Type: Cow’s milk
  • Texture: Smooth and creamy
  • Flavor: Mild, salty, slightly tangy
  • Color: Pale yellow to orange
  • Type: Processed cheese blend

About American Cheese

American cheese is a processed cheese known for its smooth, melty texture and mild flavor. It’s made from a blend of milk, milk fats, solids, and other ingredients to achieve a perfect melt and creamy consistency. It became a staple in American households and diners throughout the 20th century.

Although it’s often seen as a simple cheese, American cheese shines when melted — making it ideal for burgers, grilled cheese sandwiches, and macaroni and cheese.

Pairings

Pair American cheese with crisp pickles, tomato soup, or toasted white bread. For drinks, light lagers or a sparkling cider complement its mild, creamy taste.

Fun fact: Despite its name, American cheese was first developed by Swiss immigrants in New York in the early 1900s.
