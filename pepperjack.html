Pepper Jack Cheese

Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin:United States
  • Milk Type:Cow’s milk
  • Texture:Semi-soft
  • Fat Content:Around 30%
  • Color:Creamy white with red and green pepper flakes
  • Rind:None
  • Aging:1 to 3 months

About Pepper Jack Cheese

Pepper Jack is a flavorful American cheese that adds a spicy twist to the classic Monterey Jack. It’s made from cow’s milk and blended with chili peppers, jalapeños, and sometimes herbs, giving it a creamy yet zesty taste. Its smooth texture makes it ideal for melting, slicing, or snacking.

Pairings

Pepper Jack pairs perfectly with burgers, quesadillas, nachos, and grilled sandwiches. It also goes well with spicy meats, fresh salsa, and light beers or fruity wines that balance its heat.
