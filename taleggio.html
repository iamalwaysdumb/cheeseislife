Taleggio Cheese

Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin:Italy
  • Milk Type:Cow’s milk
  • Texture:Soft and creamy
  • Fat Content:Around 48%
  • Color:Pale yellow
  • Rind:Thin, washed rind (orange-pink)
  • Aging:6-10 weeks

About Taleggio Cheese

Taleggio is a traditional Italian cheese from the Lombardy region, known for its strong aroma but mild and tangy flavor. It has a smooth, creamy texture that melts beautifully, making it perfect for both cooking and snacking. As it ages, it develops a more complex taste with fruity and earthy notes.

Pairings

Taleggio pairs wonderfully with crusty bread, honey, figs, and walnuts. It also complements light red wines like Pinot Noir or sparkling wines. For savory dishes, it’s excellent melted in risottos, polenta, or grilled sandwiches.
