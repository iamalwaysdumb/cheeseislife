Frinault

Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin: Europe
  • Milk Type: Cow’s milk
  • Texture: Semi-firm
  • Flavor: Balanced, creamy, and slightly nutty
  • Color: Natural ivory to golden tones
  • Type: Natural cheese

About Frinault

Frinault is a traditionally crafted cheese known for its distinctive regional character and artisanal production methods. It reflects the heritage of local cheesemaking traditions and careful aging practices.

Pairings

Frinault pairs well with crusty artisan bread, seasonal fruits, toasted nuts, and a well-balanced red or white wine depending on intensity.

Fun fact: Frinault remains a favorite among culinary professionals and cheese enthusiasts worldwide.
