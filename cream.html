Cream Cheese

Quick Facts

  • Country of Origin:United States
  • Milk Type:Cow’s milk
  • Texture:Soft and smooth
  • Fat Content:Around 33%
  • Color:white
  • Rind:None
  • Aging:Fresh, not aged

About Cream Cheese

Cream cheese is a soft, mild-tasting cheese made from a mix of milk and cream. It has a smooth, spreadable texture and a rich, slightly tangy flavor. Since it’s not aged, it keeps its fresh, creamy taste and is a staple in both sweet and savory dishes.

Pairings

Cream cheese pairs perfectly with bagels, toast, crackers, and fresh fruit. It’s also used in cheesecakes, frostings, dips, and sushi rolls. Try it with smoked salmon, herbs, or honey for a delicious twist.
